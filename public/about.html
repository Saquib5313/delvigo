We Started With a Problem Everyone in Logistics Knows But Nobody Fixes

consignments that go quiet. No updates. Customers calling to ask what happened. Businesses losing time chasing their own freight. We have seen this from the inside. And we built Delvigo to do better.

How Delvigo Came to Be

Nithin Jadhav, Founder of Delvigo, did not stumble into logistics. He grew up around it. Years spent observing the industry from the ground level gave him a clear picture of what worked and what consistently failed.

The pattern was always the same - inconsistency and lack of accountability. Good logistics companies existed, but trust was hard to find. Businesses would hand over their goods and then spend the next 24 hours worrying about where they were.

Delvigo was started to break that pattern. Not just to build a logistics company, but to build a logistics brand - one with structure, consistency, and a genuine commitment to doing what it says it will do.

We are starting in Bangalore. But the vision is bigger. We want Delvigo to be the name that B2B businesses across India trust when they need freight moved without stress.

Our Mission

To make logistics reliable enough that our clients never have to follow up. We handle the freight. They focus on their business.

Our Vision

To be a large, nationally recognized logistics brand that sets a higher standard for the industry - built on trust, not just transactions.

Logistics You Don’t Have to Chase

We built Delvigo to remove uncertainty from freight movement. Real-time updates, reliable delivery, and zero follow-ups — so you can focus on growing your business.
