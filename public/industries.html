Built for Businesses That Cannot Afford to Get Logistics Wrong

Delvigo works primarily with B2B clients - manufacturers, distributors, SMEs, and e-commerce businesses that need freight partners they can rely on without micromanaging.

Industries We Serve

Manufacturing

Factory output needs to move. We make sure it does.

  • Raw material inbound from suppliers
  • Finished goods outbound to distributors and retailers
  • Inter-plant transfers and factory-to-warehouse movement
  • Bulk and heavy goods requiring proper vehicle allocation
  • Time-bound dispatches tied to production schedules

Small and Mid-Size Businesses (SMEs)

You do not need enterprise scale. You need a partner who actually shows up.

  • Regular B2B freight without high minimums or complex contracts
  • Flexible PTL options that match your order frequency
  • Single point of contact - no call center bouncing
  • Clear pricing with no surprise invoices
  • Founders who pick up when you call

E-commerce and D2C Brands

Your customer's experience starts before they open the box. It starts with delivery.

  • Last-mile delivery across Bangalore
  • B2B inventory replenishment via FTL and PTL
  • Returns handling built into the process
  • High-volume daily dispatch capability
  • Delivery confirmation and real-time updates

Distributors and Traders

Keeping supply chains moving across the network.

  • Regional stock distribution to multiple retail points
  • Scheduled PTL runs on fixed routes
  • Reliable lead times your retail network can count on
  • Documentation and compliance handled properly
