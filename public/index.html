Everything you need to know about Delvigo logistics services

FTL (Full Truckload) shipping is where one business uses the entire truck capacity for a single consignment. The truck moves directly from pickup to delivery without stops, making it faster and more secure. Delvigo offers FTL services in Bangalore for manufacturers, distributors, and high-volume freight.

FTL means your cargo fills the entire truck and goes directly to the destination. PTL (Part Truckload) means your cargo shares space with other deliverys and you pay only for what you use. FTL is faster and more secure, while PTL is more cost-effective for smaller loads.
